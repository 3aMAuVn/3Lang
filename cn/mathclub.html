Good to know: 数学俱乐部 is a great fit for families who want steady, structured maths enrichment. It works well as a standalone program or alongside tutoring for extra practice and confidence-building.

🎯 Best For

  • Students who enjoy puzzles and problem-solving
  • Children who benefit from regular maths practice
  • Students who want to build confidence and independence
  • Families looking for enrichment without weekly tutoring

🧠 What Students Do

  • Complete weekly problem sets matched to their level
  • Work through reasoning-based and multi-step questions
  • Reflect on mistakes and improve accuracy
  • Participate in optional friendly challenges

👥 Program Information

  • Primary: 2 question sets per week (10+ questions each)
  • Years 7–10: 3 question sets per week (10+ questions each)
  • Senior 数学俱乐部 (Years 11–12): higher-level review and practice up to Year 12
  • Runs during school terms
  • Online delivery — flexible and easy from home
  • Optional local or online monthly challenges
  • School holidays: Optional maths-related problem sets (1+ per week), not included in progress tracking

⭐ Benefits

  • Builds independent learning habits
  • Encourages logical thinking and perseverance
  • Tracks progress clearly over time
  • Keeps maths engaging and fun
  • Increases enjoyment and confidence in maths
